Electronics and Communication Department

Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering

About the Department of ECE

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) was established in the year 2001 with an initial intake of 60 students. Demonstrating steady growth and academic excellence, the intake was increased to 120 students in the year 2011.

The department is currently headed by Ms. Siji Sivanandan, who leads the department with a focus on quality education, innovation, and research in the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering.

Vision

To emerge as a Centre of excellence in offering quality education to produce students technically competent, socially responsible and industry ready graduates in electronics and communication engineering.

Mission

  • Ensuring effective teaching learning methodologies.
  • Inculcating creative thinking through innovative and group work exercises.
  • Developing and motivating research ability among students by establishing research linkage with leading industries.
  • Equipping faculty and students with the latest developments in Electronics and Communication and to face the challenges.

Program Educational Objectives (PEOs)

  • Apply concepts of basic electronics & communication engineering effectively.
  • Identify, analyze, and solve engineering problems in ECE.
  • Work in multidisciplinary groups and research environments.
  • Demonstrate ethics, values, confidence, and social awareness.
  • Engage in lifelong learning and intellectual pursuits.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  • Demonstrate principles of electronic circuits, digital systems, and signal processing.
  • Design applications in communication, networking, and embedded systems.
  • Develop energy-efficient and eco-friendly electronic products.

Program Outcomes (POs)

  • PO1: Apply engineering knowledge.
  • PO2: Analyze and solve complex problems.
  • PO3: Design solutions with societal and environmental concerns.
  • PO4: Conduct investigations using valid research methods.
  • PO5: Use modern tools responsibly.
  • PO6: Assess societal, health, and cultural issues.
  • PO7: Understand and apply sustainability principles.
  • PO8: Demonstrate professional ethics.
  • PO9: Work effectively in teams.
  • PO10: Communicate clearly and effectively.
  • PO11: Manage projects with financial and leadership skills.
  • PO12: Commit to lifelong learning.
