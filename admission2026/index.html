FAQ

What is the TNEA Code?

The TNEA Counselling Code for Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College is 1309. Please use this code while selecting preferences.

How do I apply?

For Counselling: Use code 1309 in TNEA.
For Direct Admission: Use the Enquiry Form above or contact our admissions office directly at +91 97908 21869.

Are hostel facilities available?

Yes, MSEC provides separate, secure, and well-maintained hostel facilities for both boys and girls with mess facilities.

Is college transport available?

While we are a "City Campus" with excellent public transport connectivity (Metro/Train/Bus), we do operate buses for specific routes.

What documents are needed for admission?

Typically, 10th & 12th Marksheets, Transfer Certificate (TC), Community Certificate, and TNEA Allotment Order (for Counselling students).
