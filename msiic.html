Meenakshi Sundararajan Innovation Incubation Centre (MSIIC)

MSIIC – Purpose & Vision

The Meenakshi Sundararajan Innovation Incubation Center (MSIIC) is established with the vision to foster an environment where young minds can transform innovative ideas into reality, driving entrepreneurship and technological advancement. MSIIC aims to be a catalyst for creating sustainable business solutions and fostering collaborations between academia, industry, and entrepreneurial ecosystems.

Objectives & Key Results (OKRs)

Fostering Innovation

Empowering breakthrough ideas with real-world impact — socially, technologically, and economically

Fuel Entrepreneurship

Offering mentorship, funding, and infrastructure to turn bold ideas into scalable ventures.

Bridge with Industry

Connecting academia to industry to ensure every innovation meets market needs.

Build Skills

Equipping students and startups with entrepreneurial and tech skills through workshops, training, and a structured 6-Semester EDI Curriculum developed in collaboration with the Wadhwani Foundation — focusing on real-world startup creation, product development, and business innovation.

MSIIC Stakeholders

Visionaries shaping strategy, governance, and long-term impact through academic and industry expertise.

Mentors and collaborators offering real-world insight, R&D opportunities, internships, and investments.

The heartbeat of MSIIC — turning academic brilliance into viable, scalable ventures.

Soccer Robot
The Soccer Robot is an autonomous or semi-autonomous machine designed to play soccer using sensors, motors, and strategic algorithms. It detects the ball, navigates the field, and attempts goals using real-time object tracking and motion control. Soccer robots promote hands-on learning in robotics, AI, and teamwork through competitive and educational platforms like RoboCup.

LandRover
The Land Rover Project is a terrain-adaptive robotic vehicle designed to navigate rough and uneven surfaces. Equipped with suspension systems and obstacle sensors, it ensures stability and mobility across challenging landscapes. Ideal for exploration, rescue operations, and educational robotics, it showcases real-world applications of mechanical and embedded systems.

E Cycle
