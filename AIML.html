🧠 Program Overview

Understanding how intelligent systems think, learn, and decide.

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI & ML) focus on building systems that analyze data, learn from patterns, and make intelligent decisions. This program introduces students to the foundations behind today’s smart technologies.

🔍 What is AI & ML?

Artificial Intelligence enables machines to mimic human intelligence such as reasoning and decision-making.

Machine Learning allows systems to learn automatically from data and improve over time.

⭐ Course Highlights
  • Concept-first learning approach
  • Industry-linked examples
  • Preparation for careers and higher studies

📘 What You Will Learn

This course provides a strong conceptual and application-oriented foundation:

Core concepts of Artificial Intelligence
Machine Learning models and learning processes
Data-driven problem solving
Pattern recognition and prediction
Ethical and responsible AI usage
Real-world case studies
