Frequently Asked Questions About NRI Engineering Admission

Everything you need to know about NRI Admissions at MSEC.

What are the eligibility criteria for NRI students?

NRI candidates must have passed 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Eligibility is based on the marks obtained in the qualifying examination as per Anna University norms.

Is there a dedicated hostel for NRI students?

Yes, we have premium air-conditioned hostel facilities for both boys and girls with international-standard dining, high-speed WiFi, and 24/7 security.

How does MSEC support startups and innovation?

MSEC has a dedicated Incubation Centre that provides workspace, seed funding advice, and mentorship for student entrepreneurs. Our "EDI" curriculum integrates entrepreneurship into engineering modules.
