Science and Humanities Department

Department of Science and Humanities

The Department of Science and Humanities was established with the vision to foster innovative ideas and research among the students and faculty members. It serves as the basis of liberal arts education and provides a platform to explore new horizons in the world of science.

The department consists of faculty from the disciplines of Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and English. Faculty members possess the ability and talent to be ideal mentors and counsellors to the students. They create the spark in students to learn more and more.

🧠 Innovator's Day – 31st May 2025

The Innovator’s Day, organized by the MSIIC club on 31st May 2025 at the MSIIC hall, provided a platform for students to showcase their project-building skills across various domains such as software applications, hardware prototypes, and environmentally sustainable solutions. A wide range of innovative ideas were presented, reflecting the students' passion for technology and real-world problem-solving. Overall, the event not only highlighted student talent but also encouraged a culture of innovation and research within the campus.
