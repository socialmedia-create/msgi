B.E. Computer Science and Engineering (Cybersecurity)

Cybersecurity

🛡️ Program Overview

Securing systems, networks, and data from cyber threats.

B.E. CSE Cybersecurity is a specialized version of Computer Science and Engineering. It includes all core computer science subjects, along with advanced training in protecting digital assets from evolving cyber threats.

Goal of the Program

To produce cybersecurity professionals capable of defending organizational infrastructure and data against sophisticated attacks using modern ethical hacking and defense techniques.

🛡️ Major Areas Covered

Core CSE subjects (Programming, OS, DBMS, Networks)
Cryptography & Encryption Techniques
Network Security
Ethical Hacking & Penetration Testing
Secure Coding Practices
Malware Analysis & Digital Forensics
Cyber Laws, Compliance & Ethics

🚀 Nature of the Course

Strong Foundation

Builds a solid programming foundation integrated with specialized security knowledge.

Specialized Focus

Focuses intensely on security techniques, defense mechanisms, and vulnerability assessment.

Suitability

Ideally suited for students interested in ethical hacking and cyber defense.
