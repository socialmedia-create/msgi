Institution's Innovation Council (IIC)

Mission

Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Govt. of India has established MHRD's Innovation Cell (MIC) to systematically foster the culture of innovation amongst all Higher Education Institutions (HEIs).

MHRD's Innovation Cell

MIC works closely with HEIs to unleash the creative energy of students and promote innovation-driven entrepreneurship. It fosters a complete ecosystem — from idea generation to successful startup graduation.

  • Pre-incubation to incubation support
  • Innovation ranking system
  • Collaborations with central & state agencies

Functions & Benefits

Exclusive Competitions

Participate in innovation initiatives and win exciting prizes & certificates.

Mentorship Access

Guidance from top business leaders and industry professionals.

Nurture Ideas

Opportunity to prototype innovative ideas and validate solutions.

Tech Experimentation

Experiment with emerging technologies and tools.

Company Visits

Visit companies & experience the entrepreneurial ecosystem in real time.
