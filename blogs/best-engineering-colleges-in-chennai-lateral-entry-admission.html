Best Engineering Colleges in Chennai | Lateral Entry Admission 2026

Lateral Entry Admission is a great opportunity for diploma and B.Sc students to join directly into the second year of an engineering degree. If you are searching for the best engineering colleges in Chennai for lateral entry admission, Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College (MSEC) offers excellent academic support, modern infrastructure, and strong placement opportunities.

Through lateral entry, students can save one academic year and continue their education in top engineering programs with industry-focused training.

Explore full details about Engineering Admission 2026.

What is Lateral Entry Admission?

Lateral entry allows eligible students to take admission directly into the second year (3rd semester) of B.E/B.Tech programs. This pathway is ideal for students who have completed:

Diploma in Engineering
B.Sc (Mathematics / Science)

This system helps students continue their higher education without starting from the first year.
