How to Reach

By Air

Chennai International Airport (MAA) is approx. 12 km from MSEC. Taxi and metro services are readily available.

By Train

Kodambakkam Railway Station is the nearest local station (walkable). Chennai Central and Chennai Egmore stations are approx. 10 km and 7 km respectively, connecting to all major cities.

By Bus/Road

The college is located on Arcot Road, Kodambakkam, and is highly accessible via public city buses and metro (Vadapalani Metro Station).
