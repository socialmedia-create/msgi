Internal Committee (IC)

The Internal Committee (IC) has been constituted in our Institution to prevent and prohibit sexual harassment of women employees and students, in accordance with the UGC guidelines.

This committee adheres to the principles outlined in:

  • 📘 Composition of Internal Committee (IC)
  • 📘 UGC Guidelines – Handbook on Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace
  • 📗 Handbook – Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal)
  • 📙 Guidelines – For prevention and redressal of harassment in Higher Education Institutions (HEIs)
Note: The IC ensures a safe, respectful and inclusive environment for all students and staff.
