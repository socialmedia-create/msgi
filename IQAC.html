Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

🌟 Vision

To ensure quality culture as the prime concern for the Higher Education Institution through institutionalizing and internalizing all the initiatives taken with internal and external support.

🎯 Objective

The primary aim of IQAC is:

  • To develop a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic action to improve the academic and administrative performance of the institution.
  • To promote measures for institutional functioning towards quality enhancement through internalization of quality culture and institutionalization of best practices.
📘 Strategies

IQAC shall evolve mechanisms and procedures for:

  • Timely, efficient and progressive performance of academic, administrative and financial tasks
  • Relevant and quality academic/research programmes
  • Equitable access and affordability for all sections of society
  • Optimization and integration of modern teaching methods
  • Credibility of assessment and evaluation process
  • Support structure and services allocation
  • Sharing research findings and networking with institutions in India and abroad
🛠️ Functions
  • Development and application of quality benchmarks
  • Parameters for academic and administrative activities
  • Creating a learner-centric environment for quality education
  • Feedback collection and analysis from all stakeholders
  • Information dissemination on quality parameters
  • Workshops, seminars, and promotion of quality circles
  • Documentation of programs leading to quality improvement
  • Acting as a nodal agency for coordinating quality activities
  • Database development and maintenance through MIS
  • Conduct of Academic & Administrative Audit and follow-up
  • Submission of AQAR as per NAAC guidelines
🏆 Benefits
  • Ensures clarity and focus in institutional quality enhancement
  • Promotes internalization of quality culture
  • Enhances coordination among institutional activities
  • Supports decision-making for improvement
  • Acts as a dynamic system for quality changes in HEIs
  • Builds systematic documentation and communication
